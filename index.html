  • JS, SQLITE, LINUX

    Reimburse track

    ReimburseTrack simplifies expense tracking by letting department managers approve spending before finance processes reimbursements. It supports receipt uploads, real-time spending dashboards, and automated email/SMS notifications at each approval stage. Running on a Raspberry Pi, it’s a secure, cost-effective way for clubs or small organizations to manage shared budgets and audits.

  • Networking, Linux

    rPi router

    Leveraged a Raspberry Pi and open-source software to bond two Starlink connections, combining bandwidth and ensuring failover redundancy. By dynamically load-balancing traffic across both links, it minimizes latency spikes and downtime—ideal for remote work, live streaming, or mission-critical connectivity. The setup includes custom QoS rules to prioritize video calls or specific applications while maintaining a seamless, enterprise-grade connection at a fraction of commercial solutions.
