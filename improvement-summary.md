## How to Use Your Improved Website

### 1. Theme Toggle
- Click the sun/moon icon in the top-right corner to switch between dark and light themes
- Your preference is automatically saved and remembered

### 2. Navigation
- Click any navigation item for smooth scrolling to that section
- The active section is highlighted in the navigation

### 3. Projects Section
- Use the filter buttons to show different types of projects
- Hover over project cards to see additional information
- Replace placeholder images with your actual project screenshots

### 4. Adding Your Content

#### YouTube Videos
Replace the placeholder divs with actual YouTube embed codes:
```html
<iframe width="100%" height="250" src="https://www.youtube.com/embed/YOUR_VIDEO_ID" frameborder="0" allowfullscreen></iframe>
```

#### Blog Posts
Update the blog section with your actual articles and images.

#### Certifications
Update the certification status badges as you complete them:
- Change `badge-in-progress` to `badge-completed`
- Update dates and descriptions

### 5. Customization
- Colors can be easily changed by modifying CSS variables in `main-improved.css`
- The green accent color (#18d26e) is maintained throughout
- Add your own project images to `assets/img/projects/`
